Recipe #1: French meat

  • Time of preparing: 1:30 Hours(except juicing meat)
  • Serves: 3-6 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 1 - 1.5 kg
Potato 2 kg
Seasoning 40g
Onion 200g
Cheese 200 g

Step 1: Slice meat, potato, cheese and onion.
Step 2: Hammmer and then juice meat.
Step 3: Prepare seasoning.
Step 4: Lay meat on the caret.
Step 5: Lay thin slices onion and potato's pieces on the meat.
Step 6:Add cheese.
Step 7: Bake it for a hour.
Step 8: Meal is done.

Recipe #2: Fried eggs

  • Time of preparing: 15 min s: 1-3 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2-6
Seasoning 10g

Step 1: Crack eggs, throw them at a pan.
Step 2: Add seasoning at your choice.
Step 3: Fry for 5 minutes per each side.
Step 4: Meal is ready.

Recipe #3: Sandwich

  • Time of preparing: 7 min s: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
bread 2 slices
Seasoning 3g
Meat 2 slices
Cheese 2 slices

Step 1: Slice bread.
Step 2: Slice some meat and cheese.
Step 3: Lay meat and cheese on first bread slice and then cover it with another.
Step 4: Meal is ready.
