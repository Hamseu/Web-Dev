What to see?


Mercury

"Earth's Evil Twin"


Mercury is first planet from the sun and often called as "Earth's Evil Twin". It's because of traits of its atmosphere, acid rainings, scorching sun and temperature. Travel to Mercury will be great choice for ones who want to test their resolve.

Mars

"The Red Planet"


Mars, everyone knows "The Red Planet". This planet is everyone's dream to colonize, because it's next step of our space journey. Mars has one of the most spectacular surfaces of all planets. Some features of it even were misunderstood with Marsian pyramids.

Moon

"Big Cheese piece"


Moon, "Big Cheese piece". It's the most obvious choice, because it has the most beautiful view on the Earth and the most friendly atmosphere - non-existing one.
